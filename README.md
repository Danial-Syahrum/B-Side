# B-Side 🎧

A real-time mobile tracking platform built to help music enthusiasts catalog their album review records, filter tracklists by score metrics, and browse global community discoveries smoothly.

## 🎵 Core Features & User Experience

B-Side is built specifically for physical music collectors who want to catalog their collection, track their spins, and share their music journey with a community of like-minded enthusiasts without the clutter of mainstream social media.

### 💿 Smart Album Discovery
No manual typing required to find your music. Simply search for any record, CD, or cassette tape, and the app instantly pulls high-fidelity album artwork, tracklists, and release details directly into your interface.

### ⏱️ "Quick-Star" Logging (Ghost Reviews)
We know writing long reviews can feel like a chore when you just want to log a spin. B-Side supports frictionless logging, you can log an album with a quick star rating in less than 2 seconds, completely skipping the text commentary. The community feed automatically handles it with a clean, elegant placeholder.

### 🛒 Track Your Acquisition Channels
Keep tabs on where your music collection actually comes from. Tag each album log by its exact purchase origin:
*   🏬 **Local Record Shops** (Support brick-and-mortar culture)
*   🎪 **Artist Merch Tables** (Direct support at live gigs)
*   🌐 **Independent Digital Platforms** (Bandcamp, indie web stores)

### 👥 Real-Time Community Feed
See what other vinyl heads and tape collectors are spinning right this second. The global community timeline updates instantly without requiring you to pull-to-refresh, creating a live, shared listening room experience.

### 📊 Collection Analytics at a Glance
Visualize your collection's footprint. Instantly track your overall rating trends and see how much your listening habits are actively driving support back into local music communities and independent creators.

## FAQ

#### What is this project made for?

First and foremost, B-Side was engineered to fulfill the technical and architectural requirements for my 4th semester UiTM CSC264 individual project, allowing me to demonstrate mobile application development, cloud database integration, and live deployment.

But beyond the grading rubric, this project comes from a place of genuine personal passion for the local music scene. As a music fan, I wanted to build something that moves away from the sterile, algorithmic nature of massive streaming corporations. B-Side was created to give physical media collectors, vinyl enthusiasts, and indie gig-goers a dedicated space to catalog their collections, track where they buy their music, and foster a genuine community that supports local brick-and-mortar record shops and independent artists.
